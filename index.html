The Leading Edge Series

Humanistic Perspectives in a Technological World

The Leading Edge Series by the Ivan Allen College of Liberal Arts at Georgia Tech explores the crucial role of humanistic perspectives in a technology-driven society. Discover how technology shapes culture, human behavior, and the evolving narrative of the modern world.

The Essays

Digital Humanities

An exploration of how digital tools and humanistic inquiry converge to shape our understanding of the world.

Innovation & Society

Analyzing the impact of technological advances on societal values, ethics, and cultural expression.

Why?

This series addresses the intersection of technology, culture, and human values, highlighting the necessity of a humanistic lens in shaping technological advancements. By fusing liberal arts perspectives with technology, it offers insights into solving global challenges.

The Thinkers

Contributors include leading scholars in digital humanities, ethical technology, cultural analysis, and historical perspectives. Their work demonstrates how the liberal arts illuminate the human aspects of technological change.
